World Trade Network

Python, Numpy, Pandas, Sklearn, Matplotlib, Metworkx, Kernighan-Lin Algorithm, Girvan-Newman Algorithm, Ravasz Algorithm, Modularity-based Greedy Algorithm
  • By analyzing the network of bilateral trade in different years, the development of the world and the closeness of the connection and trade can be revealed.
  • Although it may not be as complex as a multi-node network, the relationship between nodes in WTN is very complicated. Even small changes in graph topology may have a huge impact on the overall structure of the network. Most research on WTN was completed at the beginning of the 21st century.
  • This project aims to reveal some of the key characteristics that characterize WTN, identify dominant nodes and the way they manage the network, and study the carbon trading issues and wealth gap problems derived from WTN.
